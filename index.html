What?

This is a simple scoreboard for the 2009 Jordan, Jesse, Go! high five contest. If you don't know what that is then you should start listening to Jordan, Jesse, Go! right now.

How?

The scoreboard searches flickr for all photos tagged with 'highfive09' and sorts them out by user. Initially all photos contribute one point to the users total score; however, if Jesse comments on the photo with [-1], [+1], or [+2] the score for that photo will be updated accordingly.

Why?

For maximum fun.
